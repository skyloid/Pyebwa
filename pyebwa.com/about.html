About Pyebwa

Connecting Families, Preserving Heritage

Our Mission

At Pyebwa, we believe every family has a story worth preserving. Our mission is to provide families with the tools they need to document, share, and celebrate their unique heritage. We're building bridges between generations, ensuring that precious memories and family histories are never lost.

Our Values

Our Story

2023

Pyebwa was born from a simple idea: families should have an easy way to preserve their stories.

Early 2024

We launched our beta version, working closely with Haitian families to build the perfect platform.

Mid 2024

Expanded to support multiple languages and cultures, making Pyebwa accessible to more families.

Today

Thousands of families use Pyebwa to connect, share stories, and preserve their heritage.
