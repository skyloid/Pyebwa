If you're seeing "Immediate auth user: null" or your family tree isn't loading after login, try these solutions:

Option 1: Wait for Sync (Recommended)

After logging in, the app will automatically retry authentication up to 15 times over ~30 seconds. You should see a message saying "Syncing authentication..."

Just wait on the loading screen - it usually resolves within 10-15 seconds.

Option 2: Manual Auth Trigger

If waiting doesn't work, use our manual trigger tool:

Manual Auth Trigger

This tool will force Firebase to sync your authentication state.

Option 3: Full Debug

For persistent issues, use our comprehensive debugger:

Auth Debugger

This shows detailed information about your authentication state and provides multiple recovery options.

Option 4: Clear and Restart

As a last resort, clear all data and start fresh:

Firebase Auth Recovery

This tool can clear all authentication data and help you log in again.

Why Does This Happen?

Firebase Authentication sometimes takes a moment to sync your login state between their servers and your browser. This is especially common with:

  • Magic link (passwordless) authentication
  • Slow or unstable internet connections
  • Browser privacy settings or extensions
  • Third-party cookies being blocked
Browser Requirements:
  • Enable third-party cookies (required by Firebase)
  • Disable ad blockers for this site
  • Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Clear cache if you've had repeated issues

Still Having Issues?

Try these steps in order:

  1. Log out completely: Go to App and click Sign Out
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies for this site
  3. Try logging in again: Login Page
  4. Wait for the "Syncing authentication..." message to complete
