Audio Recording Feature - Test Guide

The audio recording feature has been successfully implemented! This allows family members to record their stories in their own voice, creating a more personal and engaging experience.

How to Test the Audio Recording Feature:

  1. Log into your Pyebwa account at pyebwa.com/app
  2. Navigate to a family member's profile
  3. Click on the "Life Stories" tab
  4. Click "Add Story" button
  5. Choose a story template or start with a blank story
  6. Fill in the story title and content
  7. In the "Audio Recording" section, click "Start Recording"
  8. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  9. Speak your story (max 5 minutes)
  10. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  11. Listen to the preview and click "Save Story"

Troubleshooting:

  • Recording won't start: Make sure you're using HTTPS (secure connection)
  • Audio won't upload: Check your internet connection and try again
