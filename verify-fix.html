✅ All Issues Fixed!

We've successfully fixed multiple issues that were preventing your family tree from displaying:

1. Missing updateFamilyMember Function

Added the updateFamilyMember function that was being called but not defined. This was causing stories, privacy settings, and other updates to fail.

2. Tree Rendering Functions Not Exported

Fixed renderFamilyTree and renderTreeNode functions not being globally accessible. These are now properly exported and can be called by the app.

How to Verify Everything is Working:

  1. Clear your browser cache
    • Chrome/Edge: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
    • Select "Cached images and files"
    • Click "Clear data"
  2. Log in to your account at rasin.pyebwa.com/app
  3. You should now see:
    • ✅ Your family tree displayed correctly
    • ✅ All family members visible
    • ✅ Ability to add/edit members
    • ✅ Audio recording for stories working
    • ✅ Privacy settings saving properly
Important: If you still don't see your family tree after clearing cache, try:
  • Using an incognito/private browser window
  • Trying a different browser
  • Logging out and logging back in
